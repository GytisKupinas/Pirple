How to Build the Best Sandwich Ever

Convenience – You can buy sandwiches practically anywhere

Versatility – A sandwich can contain any filling, as long as it's between bread.

Comfort – There's something nostalgic about sandwiches; we grew up eating them!

Mess-Free – They're incredibly easy to eat on the go, without spilling them everywhere.

Delicious recipe

  • Bread
  • Unsliced loaf of ciabatta
  • Mustard
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Veggies
  1. Choose a good bread. Start with an unsliced loaf of ciabatta from a local baker.
  2. Grab some mustard. After cutting the ciabatta loaf lengthwise, smear mustard on the bottom slice.
  3. Stack up the meat and cheese.
  4. Add your veggies.
  5. Put it together.
  6. Enjoy! ;)
